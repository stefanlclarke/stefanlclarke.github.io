Stefan Clarke

I am a second-year PhD student at the ORFE department at Princeton University where I am privileged to be advised by Professor Bartolomeo Stellato. My reserach is primarily in the field of mathematical optimization, and I am currently working on solving problems in mixed integer optimization, computational game theory, machine learning, and bilevel optimization. Before I was here I received my Masters in Mathematics and Statistics from the University of Oxford.
